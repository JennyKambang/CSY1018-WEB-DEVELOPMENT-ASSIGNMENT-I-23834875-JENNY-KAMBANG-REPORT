Site Report

Website Designer

During my creation of my own portfolio website, I went through many stages of emotions as I faced both solutions and problems to my coding, designs and debugging. Using the knowledge I was given by my lecturer, I was able to understand how to do coding and with the help of design templates done by the professionals, I was able to find the choice of my design for my website. However I faced many problems as I coded in both HTML and CSS. I faced my biggest problem Im my tags in my HTML where I wasn't able to figure out why it was not working just to figure it out that the position as well as the right wording resulted in it working. I too faced problems in my CSS like being unable to place my images in a grid format or in a column format. Also, I had trouble applying my work onto the github a lot of times as it resulted in me losing my codes where i had to re-do everything by committing cleanly first. However I was able to find my problems in the end and also learnt better after finding the solution. Being able to understand the basics made it much more easier to understand and do up my web designing however I was able to also understand the importance of it as I created more and more lines of error on my coding. Also I learned creating a better design for one website would also mean a lot more extra coding to do.
